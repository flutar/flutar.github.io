```
说理在各种写作中是必不可少的，而因果又是说理中不可或缺的。于是我就在下面汇总了 TPO 中常用的表示结果的一些表达，防止大家一个 so 一用到底。这个词没啥不好，但用多了自己也会觉得烦。大家有几个替换就行，不用搞的太多，多了也记不住。记得时候如果有推荐句式的最好以句子为单位来记，效率高一些。而且请大家重点注意一下各个词的使用的位置。千万不要以为有些词见过就略过不看了。
```

##### consequently 

Consequently, it is more difficult for gases to "evaporate" from them. [TPO 16-3-5]

##### therefore

Many scientists have **therefore** concluded that a sixth great mass extinction is currently in progress. [TPO 15-2-2]

##### thereby 

He **thereby** transformed an inefficient pump of limited use into a steam engine of a thousand uses. [6-1-2]

##### so that 

In the 1760s, James Watt perfected a separate condenser for the steam, **so that** the cylinder did not have to be cooled at every stroke; then he devised a way to make the piston turn a wheel and **thus** convert reciprocating (back and forth) motion into rotary motion. [TPO 6-1-2]

##### so as to 

Another generation passed before inventors succeeded in combining these ingredients, by putting the engine on wheels and the wheels on the rails, **so as to** provide a machine to take the place of the horse. [6-1-4]
